Spring 2017
I had an interview at UW for a summer program I was hoping to attend - particularly its writing seminar on the effects of tragedies on literature. ...My brother Tommy was attending UW and we spent time before the interview preparing together. Afterwards, I was sure I had bombed it (and I had), but Tommy convinced me to be glad it was over and take advantage of the fact I had actually dressed up by having a mini photo shoot around campus. This reminds me that there is always something to look forward to after something goes wrong.

  June 2018
My high school graduation was fairly uneventful. We had the 7:00pm graduation and my family thought that if they left at 6:30pm, there would still be plenty of seats. ...They were wrong. We waved frantically as I walked to my seats and saw them hanging off of the stairs, but to make up for it they ambushed me as I snuck out to the bathroom for a "quick" 25 minute photo shoot as everyone trickled out to give me a lei and whisper congratulations. These are almost all my siblings, but Tommy is currently studying abroad.

In this picture Callysta wanted to play one song so I was helping her fight her nerves and keep time.

  March 2018

I am pictured with Rocky, ...the other intern I was with at Amazon.
